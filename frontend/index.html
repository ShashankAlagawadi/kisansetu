About KisanSetu

Empowering Farmers Through Technology

Bridging the Gap Between Farmers and Markets

KisanSetu is a digital platform that connects farmers directly with buyers, eliminating the need for intermediaries. It enables transparent transactions and helps farmers choose reliable buyers through a vendor recommendation system based on ratings and trust.

By improving decision-making, ensuring fair pricing, and building trust, KisanSetu creates a more efficient and sustainable agricultural marketplace
